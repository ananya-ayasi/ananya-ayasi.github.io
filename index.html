About Me

Check out my blog!

I am an undergraduate student from College of Engineering Trivandrum with research experience in signal processing, deep learning, quantum technology and tech policy.

Apart from academia, I have demonstarted leadership and organizational skills at IEEE, Make A Difference and in multiple clubs in my college. With the experiences I have gained from working with underserved communities and from personal ordeals, I hope to bring about a trauma-informed, anti-racist, anti-oppressive & intersectional approach to my technical work.

I wish to work in voice-enabled technologies and/or autonomous driving systems in the future, with a focus on the social impact such technologies can have on humanity. So hit me up for any co-op or full time roles that you think would align with my interests. Thank you!
